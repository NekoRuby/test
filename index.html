Portfolio

Unity and Rhythm

Unity and Rhythm is a series of posters where squares of different sizes as the main objects. For “Unity”, the collection of discrete squares to form a homogeneous block to represent similarity. Meanwhile, “Rhythm” represents the idea of the lights on the theater as all eyes will be on the stage to focus on the squares.

  • Category: Poster
  • Client: Personal Project
  • Project date: July 2023

The Beauty of East Asia

“The Beauty of East Asia” based on different cultures from three countries: Japan, South Korea, and Taiwan. The imagery plays a crucial role in sharing the beauty of each country. Additionally, the photos of costumes, food, constructions, and entertainment depict the cultures of each nation. The three main colors are red, yellow, and blue. For Asians, they believe that red represents warmth with affluence, while blue is a symbol of harmony with gentleness and yellow is for luck and prosperity in daily life.

  • Category: Editorial Layout
  • Client: Culture guide for Tourists
  • Project date: December 2023

Thời Tiết Today

Thời Tiết Today (Weather Today) is a ficticious mobile weather forecast application in Vietnam. The goal of the application is to provide accurate weather information, forecasts of natural disasters up to 1 year and some sugesstions to help optimize the user’s life. The application has a modern interface, can be used on all operating systems.

  • Category: UX/UI
  • Client: Interaction Design Class Project
  • Project date: April 2024

Explore Asia In Different Ways

Explore Asia In Different Ways is a title sequence project that introduces key production and cast members in a video. The movie has a soft music background with videos related to Asia integrated and 3D elements are also included.
